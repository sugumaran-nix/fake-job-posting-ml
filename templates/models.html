Why Fraud-class F1, not overall Accuracy? The EMSCAD dataset is heavily imbalanced (~95% legitimate, ~5% fraudulent). A model that classifies everything as "Legitimate" would achieve ~95% accuracy but catch zero fraud. Fraud Recall (fraction of actual frauds correctly caught) and Fraud F1 (harmonic mean of fraud precision and recall) are the metrics that reflect real-world usefulness.

CV F1 (5-fold) is the mean fraud-class F1 across 5 cross-validation folds on the training set — a more robust estimate of generalisation than a single train/test split.
